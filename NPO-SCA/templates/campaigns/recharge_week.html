Recharge Week

A 7‑day challenge to reset your mental battery, one mode at a time.

What is Recharge Week?

This 7‑day series treats your mental energy like a battery that needs tending. Each day focuses on a different “mode” — from switching Sleep Mode ON for real rest to activating Airplane Mode for a digital detox — to help you consciously reset. Whether you are decluttering your mind during Clean Storage, moving your body for a Fast Charge, or connecting with friends during Battery Share, this week offers small, actionable steps to check your energy settings and fully power up again.

Recharge Week is designed to be accessible: you can follow along on social media, participate privately, or complete only the days that fit your schedule. The goal is not perfection — it’s noticing your needs, creating tiny moments of care, and reconnecting with yourself and others.

7‑Day “Battery Modes”

Day 1 — Sleep Mode ON

Prioritize real rest by setting a tech cut‑off time, softening your evening routine, and giving your body permission to power down.

Day 2 — Airplane Mode

Take a mini digital detox: mute notifications, step away from constant scrolling, and give your mind a quiet signal break.

Day 3 — Clean Storage

Declutter your space or mind — clear a corner of your room, tidy your backpack, or journal out thoughts taking up too much storage.

Day 4 — Fast Charge

Move your body in a way that feels doable: a short walk, stretch break, or dance session to boost energy and release stress.

Day 5 — Battery Share

Connect with someone who fills your cup — send a kind message, join a club event, or simply sit with a trusted friend.

Day 6 — Quiet Mode

Create a calm pocket of time with mindfulness, music, or creative expression so your brain can soften the noise of the week.

Day 7 — System Check

Reflect on what helped you feel more charged, what drained you, and which practices you want to keep as part of your routine.
