Weekly Connections

Feel-Good Fridays and seasonal celebrations.

Connection doesn't always require a grand gesture; often, it's built in the small, consistent moments. This section houses our recurring initiatives designed to spark joy and foster routine interaction. Whether it's a weekly challenge or a holiday check-in, these campaigns encourage students to step out of their comfort zones and engage with the community around them.

Feel-Good Friday

"Small Acts, Big Impact"

Every Friday, we issue a simple, actionable challenge to the student body. Whether it's "Say hi to someone new," "Go tech-free for an hour," or "Send a gratitude text," these micro-challenges are designed to reduce social anxiety and build habit-forming connections.

It's our way of turning the weekend into an opportunity for wellness.

Seasonal Campaigns

"Holiday Mental Health Checks"

Holidays can be joyful, but they can also be isolating. Our seasonal campaigns provide tailored advice for navigating the highs and lows of the school year. We focus on inclusive ways to celebrate that prioritize mental well-being over social pressure.

  • "Halloween with Heart" — SAD-friendly activities
  • "Gratitude in Action" — Thanksgiving wellness tips
  • Winter break check-ins and connection ideas
