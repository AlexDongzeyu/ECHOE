Mind Gym

"21 Days to Build Your Mental Muscles"

Just like physical fitness, mental resilience requires training. Our Mind Gym is a structured 3-week program designed to strengthen your psychological protective factors through daily practice.

  • Week 1: Strength - Building coping skills, emotional regulation, and healthy boundaries.
  • Week 2: Flexibility - Cultivating adaptability and a growth mindset.
  • Week 3: Endurance - Establishing consistency, gratitude, and purposeful routines.

The Routine: Each day features a quick "rep" - a small, actionable step like sending appreciation texts, journaling wins, or spending 5 minutes in nature.

Recharge Week

"7 Days to Reset Your Mental Battery"

Everyone understands what it feels like to run low on battery. This week-long campaign uses the familiar metaphor of phone battery life to make mental health concepts accessible and actionable. Each day focuses on daily habits that help you "recharge" your mental energy - one day, one small step at a time.

  • Day 1: Sleep Mode - Prioritize rest by setting a consistent bedtime and creating a calming nighttime routine.
  • Day 2: Low Power Mode - Identify energy drains by noticing what activities or thoughts leave you depleted.
  • Day 3: Airplane Mode - Disconnect to reconnect by taking intentional breaks from social media.
  • Day 4: Fast Charge - Exercise or fresh air provides a rapid boost to mood and energy.
  • Day 5: Battery Share - Connect with others by reaching out to a friend or family member.
  • Day 6: Clean Storage - Declutter your thoughts through journaling or talking with someone you trust.
  • Day 7: Fully Charged - Reflection and gratitude mark the final day.
