Wellness Campaigns

Interactive series to build resilience, flexibility, and strength.

Mental health isn't just a destination—it's a daily practice. In our Wellness Campaigns, we move beyond awareness into action. Whether you need a full reset or a daily strength-building exercise, these interactive series are designed to equip you with practical, evidence-based tools. We treat the mind like a muscle: with the right care and consistency, it gets stronger.

Mind Gym

"21 Days to Build Your Mental Muscles"

Just like physical fitness, mental resilience requires training. Our Mind Gym campaign is a structured 3-week program designed to strengthen your psychological protective factors.

  • Week 1: Strength — Building coping skills, emotional regulation, and boundaries
  • Week 2: Flexibility — Cultivating adaptability and a growth mindset
  • Week 3: Endurance — Establishing consistency, gratitude, and purposeful routines

The Routine: Daily "reps"—small, actionable steps to build a healthier mind.

Recharge Week

"7 Days to Reset Your Mental Battery"

We all know the feeling of running on 1%. Recharge Week uses the metaphor of a phone battery to help students manage their energy levels and prevent burnout.

Over seven days, we guide you through daily "charging habits"—from "Sleep Mode" and "Airplane Mode" (disconnecting) to "Fast Charge" (movement) and "Battery Share" (connection). It's a simple, visual guide to recognizing when you are drained and knowing exactly how to plug back in.

6 Days, 6 Therapies

"Science Meets Healing"

Therapy isn't "one-size-fits-all," and it isn't just talking on a couch. This educational series explores six evidence-based therapeutic approaches used by clinicians worldwide.

From the cognitive rewiring of CBT to the nervous system regulation of Music Therapy and the emotional expression of Art Therapy, we break down how different methods heal the brain and body.
