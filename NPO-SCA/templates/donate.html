Where Your Gift Goes

Donations made through E.C.H.O.E. are directed to related charities and community partners aligned with our mission — including youth mental health, crisis support, and community connection initiatives. When appropriate, a small portion may support program essentials to run campaigns and events.

  • Partner charities advancing youth mental health and crisis support
  • Community groups fostering belonging and social connection
  • Essential program needs that enable outreach and events

Every contribution — large or small — helps reduce isolation and amplify voices that deserve to be heard.
