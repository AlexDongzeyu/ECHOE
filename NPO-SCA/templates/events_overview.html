ECHOE of the Gifted Hearts

November 8, 2025 - Hosted by E.C.H.O.E

Our ECHOE of the Gifted Hearts event was a vibrant success, bringing teens together to fulfill our mission: breaking social isolation and fostering a supportive community.

  • Guest Presentation: Dianna Jiang from Gifted People Services (GPS) shared valuable insights on autism awareness and inclusion.
  • Interactive Connections: "Social Circle Bingo" and the fast-paced "Social Circle Challenge" sparked new conversations.
  • Fundraising: Bake Sale Café, Raffle Booth, and Art Wall Fundraiser—all donations supported GPS.
  • Community Closing: Celebratory group photo and Connection Pledge Wall.

Thank you to everyone who made this happen—your presence matters.
