ECHOE of the Gifted Hearts

November 8, 2025 - Hosted by E.C.H.O.E.

Our ECHOE of the Gifted Hearts event brought teens together to break social isolation and foster community. In partnership with Gifted People Services (GPS), we raised awareness for autism and mental health while creating meaningful connections.

Event Highlights:

  • Guest Presentation: Dianna Jiang from GPS shared insights on autism awareness.
  • Interactive Connections: "Social Circle Rings" helped participants meet new people.
  • Fundraising: Bake Sale, Raffle Booth, and Art Wall Fundraiser supported GPS.
  • Connection Pledge Wall: Attendees made personal promises to support mental health.

As E.C.H.O.E. - Empathy, Connection, Hope, Outreach, and Empowerment - we created a safe space where every teen felt seen and valued.
