Volunteer with E.C.H.O.E.

Join our movement to make every voice heard.

Help us fight against social isolation and ensure no student feels alone. Research shows volunteering itself is a protective factor - helping others increases your own sense of purpose and connection.

We're looking for volunteers who can:

Assist with Events

Help set up, facilitate activities, and create welcoming environments at our community gatherings.

Reply to Letters

After training, join our peer responders who read and reply to letters through Share Your Story.

Create Content

Design graphics, write captions, or produce videos that spread our message on social media.

Spread Awareness

Become an E.C.H.O.E. ambassador by sharing resources in your school and community.

All volunteers receive training and ongoing support from our team.
