Volunteer with E.C.H.O.E.

Join our movement to make every voice heard.

Volunteering is a great way to get involved in your community while making a real difference in the lives of your peers. Help us fight against social isolation and ensure that no student has to feel alone.

Research shows that volunteering itself is a protective factor — helping others increases your own sense of purpose and connection.

We are looking for volunteers who can:

Assist with In-Person Events

Help us set up, facilitate activities, and create welcoming environments at our community gatherings. Event volunteers should be comfortable interacting with peers and committed to maintaining inclusive, judgment-free spaces.

Help Reply to Anonymous Letters

After completing our peer responder training, volunteers can join our team of compassionate responders who read and reply to letters submitted through our Share Your Story platform. This role requires empathy, good boundaries, and commitment to our response guidelines.

Create Social Media Content

Share your creativity by designing graphics, writing captions, or producing video content that spreads our message. Content volunteers work with our team to ensure all posts align with our mission and meet quality standards.

Spread Awareness

Become an E.C.H.O.E. ambassador by sharing our resources, telling others about our programs, and representing our values in your daily interactions in your school and community.

If interested, please apply through the volunteer form below. All volunteers receive training appropriate to their role and ongoing support from our team.
