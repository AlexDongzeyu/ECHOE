Privacy Policy

At Light in Silence, we highly value your privacy. Our service is built on the foundation of anonymity and trust.

Our Commitment

  • We do not collect, store, or track your name.
  • We do not collect, store, or track your location.
  • We do not collect, store, or track your IP address.
  • We will never sell or share your data.

Information Collection

We only collect the following information:

  • The content of letters you choose to share
  • If you choose to provide an anonymous email for replies, we will only use it to respond to your letter

Data Security

All submitted letters are stored on secure servers, and only authorized volunteers can access the content for the purpose of replying.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies solely to provide basic functionality. These cookies are not used to track you or identify who you are.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us through our contact page.
