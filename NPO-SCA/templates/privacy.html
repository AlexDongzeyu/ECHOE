Privacy Policy

At E.C.H.O.E, we highly value your privacy. Our service is built on the foundation of anonymity and trust.

Our Commitment

  • We do not ask for your real name.
  • We do not ask for your precise location.
  • We do not build profiles about you or sell your data.

Information Collection

We collect only what we need to run the service safely:

  • The content of letters you choose to share.
  • If you choose to provide an anonymous email for replies, we will only use it to respond to your letter.
  • A random anonymous ID cookie (such as echoe_anon) so we can link your letters to your inbox and gently rate‑limit spam.

Data Security

All submitted letters are stored on secure servers, and only authorized volunteers can access the content for the purpose of replying.

Cookies & Anti‑Abuse Protection

Our website uses minimal cookies solely to provide basic functionality. One cookie is used to create an anonymous “inbox” for your letters and to prevent spam or abusive automation. This cookie does not contain your name, email, or other direct identifiers.

To protect the platform from automated attacks, we may also use third‑party spam‑protection tools such as Google reCAPTCHA on some forms. These services may collect technical information (like your browser type or IP address) directly from your device to determine whether a request is legitimate. Their use is covered by their own privacy policies (for example, see Google’s Privacy Policy and Terms of Service).

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us through our contact page.
