Self-Care & Wellness

Risk & Protective Factors

Teens today face challenges like loneliness, bullying, academic pressure, and family or financial struggles. We address these by building:

  • Peer connectedness & inclusive clubs
  • Safe, low-stimulus meetups
  • Coping & mindfulness skills
  • Supportive adults & accessible communication

Mindfulness & Meditation

  • Headspace app
  • Calm app
  • Insight Timer
  • YouTube guided meditations

Physical Wellness

  • Regular exercise routine
  • Nature walks
  • Yoga practice
  • Healthy sleep schedule
