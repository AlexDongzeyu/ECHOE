About Light in Silence

Where Your Words Find Light

Our Mission

Light in Silence is a Canadian non-profit mental health platform dedicated to bridging the gap between online and offline support systems. We believe that every voice deserves to be heard, and every story has the power to heal.

Our innovative approach combines traditional letter-writing with modern technology, creating a safe space where individuals can share their experiences, receive compassionate responses, and find the support they need.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental health support is accessible, compassionate, and free from judgment. Through our dual approach of online platforms and physical mailboxes, we're creating multiple pathways to connection and healing.

Our goal is to normalize conversations around mental health and provide immediate support to those who need it, regardless of their location, circumstances, or comfort level with traditional therapy.

Our Values

Anonymity & Privacy

Your identity is protected. We never collect personal information unless you choose to provide it, and all communications are secure.

Compassionate Response

Every letter receives a thoughtful, empathetic response from trained volunteers who understand the importance of your trust.

Community Support

We're building a community of care where everyone supports each other through life's challenges, big and small.

Timely Response

We commit to responding to all letters within 48 hours, because we know that when you reach out, you need to know someone is listening.

Our Unique Approach

What makes Light in Silence different is our combination of traditional and modern communication methods, ensuring that everyone can find a way to connect that feels comfortable for them.

Online Platform

  • Instant submission and responses
  • AI-powered immediate support
  • Easy reply tracking with codes
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • 24/7 availability

Physical Mailboxes

  • Handwritten letter option
  • Community-based locations
  • No technology required
  • Traditional "tree hole" concept
  • Accessible to everyone

Our Team

Light in Silence is powered by a dedicated team of mental health advocates, technology professionals, and trained volunteers who believe in the power of human connection.

A Message from Our Volunteers

"Every letter we receive is a privilege to read. We're honored that people trust us with their most vulnerable moments, and we take that responsibility seriously. Our volunteers undergo extensive training in empathetic communication, crisis recognition, and resource referral to ensure every response is helpful and appropriate."

Our Volunteer Commitment

Professional Training

All volunteers complete comprehensive training in mental health support, active listening, and crisis intervention.

Background Checks

Every volunteer undergoes thorough background screening to ensure the safety and trustworthiness of our platform.

Ongoing Support

Our volunteers receive continuous training updates and have access to supervision and debriefing sessions.

Serving Canada

Based in Canada, we understand the unique challenges faced by Canadians accessing mental health support. Our platform is designed with Canadian privacy laws, cultural context, and resource networks in mind.

We're working to establish physical mailbox locations in community centers, libraries, and other safe spaces across all provinces and territories, ensuring that geography is never a barrier to receiving support.
