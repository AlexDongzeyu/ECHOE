# ECHOE Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity
**Mission:** To provide a calming, inclusive, and professional space for mental health support.
**Keywords:** Empathy, Connection, Hope, Outreach, Empowerment.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
*   **Deep Indigo:** `#2A3D66` - Used for primary text, headings, and strong contrast elements. Represents stability and depth.
*   **Warm Pearl White:** `#FDFBF7` - Background base, creating a softer, paper-like reading experience compared to harsh pure white.

### Brand Accent Colors
*   **Brand Orange:** `#F8D29C` - Warmth, energy, and optimism.
*   **Brand Blue:** `#9FC5CB` - Calmness, serenity, and trust.
*   **Brand Green:** `#ADCBA4` - Growth, healing, and nature.

## Typography

### Headings
*   **Font:** `DM Serif Display`
*   **Usage:** Main section headers, hero titles. Adding a touch of elegance and humanity.

### Body Text
*   **Font:** `Nunito`
*   **Usage:** Main content, paragraphs, UI elements. Rounded sans-serif for approachability and readability.

### Handwriting / Accents
*   **Font:** `Patrick Hand`
*   **Usage:** Quotes, notes, and personal touches to simulate a handwritten feel.

## visual Language

### Texture & Depth
*   **Paper Textures:** The design uses subtle paper texture backgrounds to ground the digital experience in something tactile and familiar.
*   **Translucent Layers:** Content sections use white backgrounds with reduced opacity (e.g., `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.9)`) to let the texture show through, creating depth without clutter.

### Imagery
*   **Style:** Gentle, illustrative, and inclusive.
*   **Mascot:** The ECHOE Cat is used as a friendly guide throughout the interface.
*   **Motifs:** Nature elements (flowers, butterflies) and light effects to symbolize hope and growth.

### Animation
*   **Philosophy:** Smooth, calming transitions. No jarring movements.
*   **Technology:** GSAP is used for high-fidelity animations like floating elements and gentle fades.
