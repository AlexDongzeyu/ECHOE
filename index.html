How It Works

Your journey to healing through our comprehensive support system

Anonymous & Safe

Share your story completely anonymously. No registration required for letter submission. Your privacy and safety are our top priorities.

AI-Powered Support

Get immediate responses from our trained AI companion, designed to provide supportive, practical, or reflective guidance based on your needs.

Human Connection

Connect with trained volunteers who provide personalized, empathetic responses to help you on your healing journey.

Crisis Detection

Our system automatically detects crisis situations and provides immediate access to professional support resources.

Multi-Channel Support

Access support through our website, physical mailboxes, or anonymous email - whatever feels most comfortable for you.

Canadian Focus

Designed specifically for Canadians, with access to local crisis resources, support services, and culturally relevant assistance.
