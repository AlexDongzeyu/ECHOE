Self-Care Strategies

Daily Wellness

  • Maintain regular sleep schedule
  • Stay physically active
  • Eat nutritious meals
  • Practice gratitude

Stress Management

  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Meditation or mindfulness
  • Progressive muscle relaxation
  • Time in nature

Social Connection

  • Stay connected with loved ones
  • Join support groups
  • Volunteer in your community
  • Seek professional help when needed

Warning Signs

  • Persistent sadness or anxiety
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Changes in sleep or appetite
  • Thoughts of self-harm

When to Seek Professional Help

Professional mental health support can make a significant difference. Consider reaching out if you're experiencing persistent difficulties that impact your daily life.

Family Doctor

Your first point of contact for mental health concerns and referrals

Counselor/Therapist

Professional talk therapy and coping strategy development

Psychiatrist

Medical doctor specializing in mental health and medication management

Community Services

Local mental health programs and support groups in your area
