Physical Mailboxes

Our "tree hole" mailboxes are located in community centers across Canada. You can write a handwritten letter and drop it in one of these secure locations.

Coming Soon: Physical Locations

We're currently setting up physical mailbox locations across Canada. Check back soon for updates!

How it works:

  • Write your letter by hand
  • Fold it and place it in the secure mailbox
  • Include your preferred reply method (if any)
  • A volunteer will collect and respond within 48 hours
